# Module 4 — Launch comms kit

## Summary
Develop and deliver a comprehensive communications kit for the launch of Module 4.

## Definition of Done
undefined

## Acceptance Criteria
- The communications kit includes a press release.
- The communications kit includes a media advisory.
- The communications kit includes a set of social media posts.
- The communications kit includes a FAQ document.
- The communications kit includes a presentation deck.
- The communications kit is reviewed and approved by the marketing team.

## Handoff Actions
1. Provide the communications kit to the marketing team for review.
2. Schedule a meeting with the marketing team to discuss the kit and gather feedback.
3. Ensure all materials in the kit are up-to-date and accurate.
