# Map bounty / code-fix sources & filters

## Summary
Identify and list 2 to 4 relevant bounty or code-fix marketplaces or repositories that align with TypeScript, Next.js, and AI skills, including appropriate filters to match payout targets.

## Definition of Done
A documented list of 2 to 4 marketplaces or repositories is created, each with specified filters that align with TypeScript, Next.js, and AI skill requirements and payout expectations. The list is reviewed and approved by the product team.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 2 and no more than 4 marketplaces or repositories are identified.
- Each marketplace or repository supports bounties or code-fix tasks.
- Filters are defined for each source to target TypeScript, Next.js, and AI-related tasks.
- Filters include payout targets or ranges to ensure alignment with expected compensation.
- The compiled list is clear, concise, and ready for use in automation or manual task sourcing.

## Handoff Actions
1. Share the documented list with the automation development team.
2. Provide any necessary access or credentials to the identified marketplaces or repositories.
3. Coordinate with the product team for any clarifications or adjustments based on the list.
