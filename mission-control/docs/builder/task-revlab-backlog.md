# Create initial Revenue Lab experiments backlog

## Summary
Create an initial backlog of at least 5 scored Revenue Lab experiments, including topics such as pricing, YouTube funnel, AI spend dashboard, and bounty automations.

## Definition of Done
A backlog containing at least 5 experiments is created and scored. Each experiment includes a clear title, description, scoring criteria, and relevant tags. The backlog is documented and shared with the Revenue Lab team for review.

## Acceptance Criteria
- At least 5 experiments are listed in the backlog.
- Each experiment has a clear and concise title.
- Each experiment includes a detailed description.
- Each experiment is scored based on predefined criteria (e.g., impact, effort, confidence).
- Experiments cover the specified topics: pricing, YouTube funnel, AI spend dashboard, bounty automations, and at least one additional relevant experiment.
- The backlog is documented in a shared location accessible to the Revenue Lab team.
- The backlog is reviewed and acknowledged by the Revenue Lab team.

## Handoff Actions
1. Share the backlog document/link with the Revenue Lab team.
2. Schedule a review meeting to discuss the initial backlog.
3. Gather feedback from the team and update the backlog as needed.
