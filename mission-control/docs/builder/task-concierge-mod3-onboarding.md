# Module 3 — Onboarding emails + portal

## Summary
Develop and implement onboarding emails and a portal for Module 3 of the Concierge service.

## Definition of Done
undefined

## Acceptance Criteria
- Onboarding emails are sent to new users upon registration.
- The onboarding portal is accessible from the user dashboard.
- The portal includes all necessary onboarding materials and resources.
- The onboarding process is user-friendly and intuitive.
- All onboarding content is localized and accessible in multiple languages.

## Handoff Actions
1. Provide the design mockups for the onboarding portal.
2. Ensure all email templates are ready for deployment.
3. Coordinate with the marketing team for email content approval.
4. Schedule a user testing session for the onboarding feature.
