# Define Clawbot Concierge offer & tiers

## Summary
Create a comprehensive one-pager document defining the Clawbot Concierge offer, including audience segmentation, detailed descriptions of Tier 1 and Tier 2 service tiers, pricing models, and approval flags. This document will serve as the foundational reference for sales, marketing, and product teams to align on the offer details and customer targeting.

undefined

## Acceptance Criteria
- The one-pager clearly identifies and describes the target audience segments for Clawbot Concierge.
- Tier 1 service is described as a done-for-you installation with clear scope and deliverables.
- Tier 2 service is described as a Mission Control starter package with defined features and limitations.
- Pricing for each tier is clearly stated and includes any conditions or discounts.
- Approval flags are defined to guide sales and operational decision-making.
- The document is reviewed and approved by product, sales, and finance stakeholders.

## Handoff Actions
1. Draft the initial one-pager based on existing product and sales input.
2. Circulate draft to product management, sales leadership, and finance for feedback.
3. Incorporate feedback and finalize the document.
4. Store the final one-pager in the Mission Control documentation repository.
5. Notify all relevant teams (sales, marketing, product) of the new offer definition.
